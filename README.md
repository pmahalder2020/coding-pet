# coding-pet
A browser-based digital pet that grows as you code. Log sessions and tasks to earn XP and evolve your companion through 6 stages.

Log your coding sessions and completed tasks to earn XP. Your pet evolves through 6 stages — from a humble egg to an all-powerful deity — based on how consistently you show up.
Usage
Download TimerEgg.html and open it in any browser. No setup, no dependencies.
How it works

Log time — slide to your session length and submit. Every minute ≈ , 0.6 XP.
Log tasks — check off what you built or solved. Harder tasks give more XP.
